12 Months of Bad CGI Sharks

12 Months of Bad CGI Sharks   
2023
Video 
Premiere Pro, Garageband

When tasked with making a video depicting twelve months of something, the first subject that came to my mind was bad CGI sharks. As a fan of Syfy shark movies, I thought that this would be a strange and quirky video. Also, I knew that there were at least twelve horrible shark movies from which I could choose. I compiled all of the clips in Premiere Pro along with an extended version of the “(The Ballad of) Sharknado”. I extended the Sharknado theme song in Garageband. The thumbnail image was created using Midjourney with the prompt, "CGI sharks flipping through a calendar ". 

    The video contains clips from the following movies listed in order in which they appear in the video: 
  • Avalanche Sharks
  • MechaShark
  • DinoShark
  • Atomic Shark
  • 5 Headed Shark Attack
  • Trailer Park Sharks
  • Sand Sharks
  • Sharknado 3: Oh Hell No!
  • Sharktopus
  • Ghost Shark
  • Dam Sharks
  • Santa Jaws

Song played in the background is “(The Ballad of) Sharknado” by Quint.
