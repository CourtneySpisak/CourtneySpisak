A Life Well Lived

Link to A Life Well Lived Game
A Life Well Lived   
2023
Game, Digital Illustration Game Sprites, Narrative 
Photoshop, Unity

This is a game built by me and the members of my group from the SJSU Game Development Club. This was made for the Global Game Jam with the theme of Roots. My team had three days to design and create the entire game. I contributed all of the art elements which included about sixty different tree sprites, flower sprites, and backgrounds. In addition to the art, I also helped write one of the branches of the story.
